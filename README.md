# My Digital Twin — Personal Agent Kernel

This is a personal agent kernel — a portable, structured encoding of my unique knowledge, decision-making frameworks, and operating principles.

It is built on the observation that AI systems are becoming increasingly agentic, and the role of high-leverage humans is shifting: not to compete with agents task-by-task, but to **design, guide, and deploy systems that reflect their own judgment**.

This kernel encodes *how I think* — so that an AI can reason, decide, and act the way I would.

---

## What This Is

A distillation of my most valuable, hard-won capabilities into a form that is both **human-legible** and **agent-executable**.

This is not a generic role description or a resume. Every skill codified here represents a specific mental model or operating procedure I use to solve real problems.

The kernel is organized across four dimensions:

- **Skills** — Codified versions of my specific decision frameworks and operating procedures
- **Prompts** — Structured prompt templates designed to invoke each skill effectively
- **Templates** — Reusable document formats aligned to my workflows
- **Workflows** — Step-by-step procedures reflecting how I approach recurring tasks

---

## Core Philosophy

My thinking is consistently shaped by a few foundational principles:

| Lens | How I Apply It |
|------|----------------|
| **First Principles** | Strip away assumptions. Rebuild solutions from fundamental truths, not industry convention. |
| **Potential Over Pedigree** | What someone can become matters more than where they've been. |
| **Guided Discovery** | Lead people to answers through questions, not by providing the answer directly. |
| **Inevitability & Leverage** | Change is structural, not cyclical. Get ahead of it, then scale it. |
| **Learning > Speed** | Invest in the mental model, not just the immediate fix — until constraints demand otherwise. |

## The 5 Core Skills

### 1. Solution Strategy Formulation
**When to use:** A new feature, problem, or business requirement needs a clear solution strategy mapped out.

Covers the full arc from *identifying stakeholder triggers and primary pain points* → *as-is analysis* → *gathering constraints* → *generating multiple solution pathways* → *feasibility assessment* → *stakeholder communication*.

→ [`skills/solution-strategy-formulation.md`](skills/solution-strategy-formulation.md)

---

### 2. Innovation & Cross-Domain Solution Design
**When to use:** Existing solutions are inadequate, unavailable, or the problem requires fundamental innovation — not optimization.

The key differentiator from Skill #1: this creates entirely **new options** rather than selecting from existing ones. Uses first-principles decomposition and deliberate cross-domain exploration to synthesize genuinely novel solutions.

→ [`skills/innovation-cross-domain-design.md`](skills/innovation-cross-domain-design.md)

---

### 3. Talent Discovery
**When to use:** Evaluating candidates, building hiring rubrics, or assessing team members for complex, ambiguous roles.

Defines a clear heuristic framework for identifying high-potential individuals who think well over those who merely know a lot. Includes Green Flags, Red Flags, a litmus test methodology, and the core Potential vs. Pedigree decision philosophy.

→ [`skills/talent-discovery.md`](skills/talent-discovery.md)

---

### 4. Mentorship & Unblocking
**When to use:** A team member is stuck, tunnel-visioned, facing a blank page, or needs structural feedback during a peer review.

Uses a Socratic, guided-discovery model. Covers how to resolve the "Blank Page" scenario, break the "Rabbit Hole" fixation, apply the "Tell vs. Guide" decision matrix, and conduct reviews that teach, not just control quality.

→ [`skills/mentorship-and-unblocking.md`](skills/mentorship-and-unblocking.md)

---

### 5. Agentic Workflow Advocacy & Change Management
**When to use:** Facing organizational resistance, fear of job loss, or management anxiety around AI adoption.

Uses the *Inevitability Principle* and the *Digital Twin reframe* to convert fear of replacement into a compelling vision for exponential personal leverage and monetizable scale. Covers dismantling standard objections (hallucination, data security, cost, control) and outlines the tactical onboarding steps.

→ [`skills/agentic-workflow-advocacy.md`](skills/agentic-workflow-advocacy.md)
